TABS LORE

WOBBLERS


WOBBLERS ARE THE PRIMARY INHABITANTS OF THE TABS WORLD. THEY HAVE WANDERED IT FOR EONS PAST. FROM SINCE THE AGE OF TRIBAL TO THE DARK AWAKENING.

THEIR CIVIZATIONS HAVE RISEN AND FALLEN. SPREAD OUT ACROSS THE CONTINENT, THEY THRIVE.

THE LIFETIME OF WOBBLERS ARE QUITE VARIED. IT IS THEORIZED THAT SOME WOBBLERS LIVE SO LONG BECAUSE OF CHANNELING SOME KIND OF MAGIC FROM BEYOND THE PLANE.
